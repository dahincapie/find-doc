About

Military surgeon in the British Army, born in Cork, Ireland with medical degree from the University of Edinburgh Medical School. Inspector General (equivalent to Brigadier General) in charge of military hospitals, the second highest medical office in the British Army. Performer of the first recorded caesarean section by a European in Africa in which both the mother and child survived the operation.
